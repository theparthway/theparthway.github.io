Pranav Shastry

Founder and CEO

“There is no greater force in nature than human will coupled with faith” Everyone has a zeal to become financially independent but only a few achieve it. This may be due to various reasons like lack of proper knowledge, not knowing where to invest, lack of time, etc. Pranav very strongly believes that Savers are Losers and one can easily become financially independent by investing wisely. He co-founded and bootstrapped Dr. Rise in 2021 to provide a platform for people to invest and to make them understand why they need to invest. Pranav is currently pursuing B Tech in Artificial Intelligence. Talking about his hobbies, he loves spending his leisure time outdoors exploring new places, connecting with new people, and playing sports.
