Pranav Shastry

Founder and CEO

“There is no greater force is nature than human will coupled with faith”
Everyone has a zeal to become financially independent but only a few achieve it. This maybe due to various reasons like lack of proper knowledge, not knowing where to invest, lack of time etc.
Pranav very strongly believes that Savers are Losers and one can easily become financially independent by investing wise. He co-founded and bootstrapped Dr Rise in 2021 to provide a platform for people to invest and to make them understand why one needs invest.
Pranav is currently pursuing B Tech in Artificial Intelligence from Mahindra University. Talking about his hobbies, he loves spending his leisure time outdoors exploring new places, connecting with new people and playing sports.
